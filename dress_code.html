Dress Code

Traditional & Elegant

We would love for our guests to be part of our magical day by dressing in traditional and elegant attire. Please see the dress guidelines below to help you choose your outfit:

For Ladies

We encourage you to wear sarees, lehengas, or any other traditional Indian attire that makes you feel beautiful. Feel free to embrace vibrant colors, intricate embroidery, and elegant jewelry to match the festive spirit of our wedding.

For Gentlemen

We invite you to wear a sherwani, kurta-pajama, or a formal suit for our special day. Traditional Indian wear is preferred, and vibrant colors or neutrals are both suitable choices. Feel free to accessorize with a stole or turban for added elegance.

Dress Comfortably

The most important thing is for you to be comfortable and ready to celebrate with us. Please keep in mind that the ceremony will be held outdoors, so plan accordingly for the weather.

We can't wait to celebrate with you! ❤
